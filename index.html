Teasaurus FAQ

Does Teasaurus only deliver within Singapore?

Yes, currently we only deliver to customers in singapore

What are the Delivery options available?

Orders are fulfilled via Singpost standard delivery, and should arrive at your doorstep within four working days. Currently, express deilvery is unavailable, but we are working on making it a checkout option.

How much am i paying for delivery?

Delivery is free for purchases above SGD$25 and for all our tea sets. For orders below $25, a delivery fee of $2 will be incured
